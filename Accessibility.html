Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: May 2, 2026  |  Fully Loaded Websites  |  Asheville, NC
Our Commitment

Fully Loaded Websites is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards to ensure our website is usable by as many people as possible.

Conformance Status

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities. Conformance with these guidelines helps make the web more user-friendly for everyone.

We are actively working to increase accessibility and usability of our website. If you find anything that is not fully accessible to you, please contact us.

Measures We Take

Fully Loaded Websites takes the following measures to ensure accessibility:

  • Include accessibility as part of our web design and development practice
  • Use semantic HTML to ensure proper document structure
  • Provide text alternatives for non-text content
  • Ensure sufficient color contrast throughout our site
  • Make all functionality available via keyboard navigation
  • Avoid content that flashes more than three times per second
  • Ensure form fields have descriptive labels
  • Use clear and consistent navigation across pages
  • Ensure links have descriptive, meaningful text
  • Test our website with screen readers and accessibility tactics
Known Limitations

Despite our best efforts, some content on our website may not yet fully conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA. We are actively working to address these issues. Known limitations may include:

  • Some older PDF documents may not be fully accessible
  • Third-party embedded content (such as video players or social media widgets) may not meet all accessibility standards
  • Some complex interactive elements may have limited screen reader support

We are actively working to resolve these issues in a timely manner.

Assistive Technologies Supported

Our website aims to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen readers (including NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Voice recognition software
  • High contrast display modes
Feedback & Contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you experience any barriers accessing content on our site, or if you need information in an alternative format, please contact us. We will try to respond within 2 business days.
